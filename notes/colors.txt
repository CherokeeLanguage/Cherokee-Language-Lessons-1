=== Mooney:
black: gvnigei
blue: sagonigei
brown: wodigei
gray: sagonigei iyusdi = gray/bluish gray
green: ijei-yusdi
purple: dehaligei
red: gigagei
roan: uneg iyusdi
dun: ujv lahidvi - (soot colored)
crimson: gigagei, uwodigei, usgasda 
white: unega
yellow: dalonigei
vermillion: ela wadi (paint clay)
